Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28 April 2026  ·  Version 1.0

This policy explains what personal data Datajagers collects, why, and what your rights are under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

Controller

The data controller responsible for this website is:
Datajagers
Operated by Wouter Jagers
Email: wouter.jagers@datajagers.nl

Data we collect

We collect personal data in exactly one place: the contact form on this website. When you submit the form, we receive:

  • Name — to address you personally
  • Email address — to reply to your enquiry
  • Service interest — to understand your needs
  • Message — the content of your enquiry

We do not collect any other personal data. There are no tracking cookies, advertising pixels, or analytics scripts on this website.

Purpose and legal basis

We process your contact form data for one purpose: responding to your enquiry and, where relevant, entering into a professional engagement.

The legal basis is Article 6(1)(b) GDPR (processing necessary for the performance of or steps prior to a contract) and, where no contract follows, Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interest in responding to business enquiries).

Data processor: Netlify

This website is hosted and form submissions are stored by Netlify, Inc. (512 2nd Street, Suite 200, San Francisco, CA 94107, USA). Netlify acts as our data processor under a Data Processing Agreement.

Because Netlify is a US-based company, your data is transferred to the United States. This transfer is covered by Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission, providing an adequate level of protection for your data.

For details on Netlify's data handling, see netlify.com/privacy.

Retention period

Contact form submissions are retained for a maximum of 12 months from the date of submission, after which they are deleted. If a professional engagement results, relevant communications may be retained for up to 7 years as required by Dutch administrative and fiscal law.

Cookies

This website does not set any analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies. The only browser storage used is a single localStorage key (dj_privacy_accepted) to remember that you have acknowledged this privacy notice. This key contains no personal data and is not transmitted to any server.

Your rights

Under the GDPR you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of access (Art. 15) — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16) — correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17) — request deletion of your data
  • Right to restriction (Art. 18) — limit processing in certain cases
  • Right to object (Art. 21) — object to processing based on legitimate interest
  • Right to portability (Art. 20) — receive your data in a machine-readable format

To exercise any of these rights, email wouter.jagers@datajagers.nl. We will respond within 30 days.

Right to lodge a complaint

If you believe we are processing your data unlawfully, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch supervisory authority:

autoriteitpersoonsgegevens.nl
