{How It Works}

A SIMPLE, PROVEN PROCESS

Whether I’m acting as your Excel Guru or your AI Strategist, I follow a clear framework to ensure we’re building exactly what you need. By starting with your 'Why' and applying a structured Lean mindset, I make sure the 'computer-stuff' actually solves your real-world business problems.

STEP 1.

The Sparring Session

We start with your "Why." In a quick intake, I uncover what you truly want to achieve, ensuring we only invest time in work that adds real value.

STEP 2.

The Blueprint

I analyze the "mess" and map out a conceptual structure. Using Lean Six Sigma principles, I identify waste and design the most efficient path forward.

STEP 3.

The Build

Whether it's an Excel model, an AI integration, or a data flow, I build the "missing piece" rapidly. I turn the conceptual plan into a high-speed reality.

STEP 4.

THE Value Delivery

We launch, review, and refine. I provide ongoing support to ensure the solution stays relevant, works perfectly, and never ends up "on the shelf."
